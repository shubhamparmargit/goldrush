SHIPPING, DELIVERY, AND LOGISTICS POLICY

4. For domestic orders, the standard delivery timeline is fifteen days. The Company shall not be liable for any delay in delivery howsoever caused.

5. At the time of delivery, you must produce any one of the following Government-issued photo identity proofs: PAN Card, Driving License, Passport, Voter Identification Card, or Aadhaar Card. 6. Shipping and handling charges shall be as per the rates specified on the Platform at the time of order placement. The Company may offer free shipping during special promotional periods at its sole discretion. All shipping charges are in addition to the product price unless specifically stated otherwise.

The Company shall not be responsible or liable for any act, omission, negligence, misconduct, or default on the part of such logistics providers including but not limited to loss, damage, theft, delay, or mishandling of products during transit.
